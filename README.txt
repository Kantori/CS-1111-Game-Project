README

Developers: Andrew Walsh, Kevin Naddoni
---------------------------------------------------------

Purgatory is our game project for CS 1111 at the University of Virginia.

Objectives:
1. Collect as many coins as possible!
2. Enemies will hurt you if you touch them. Shoot them down with your laser pistol!
3. Play until you reach the top of the leaderboards!

Controls:
1. Use arrow keys (?,?,?,?) to travel the map.
2. Press the spacebar to shoot at enemies.


On the homepage, click on the trophy to see the top scores!
